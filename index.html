Bezier - A Montreal Grouphouse

We—Stephen: a novice bit-bashing telescope engineer, Tiffany: forager of stories, and (possibly) Liam: apprentice evoker of dreams—are starting a group house in Montreal! This will be a space for nerds and futurists to exchange ideas—or rather, for ideas to take the leap across the chasm between two minds. To this end, we call for artists, engineers and scientists, and founders and technophiles to gather at our house once per week for dinner and demo nights.

When? Sundays, starting in September.
Where? Montreal, address not yet extant.
If you're interested in being a part of this in any capacity, please scan (or click on) the QR code and fill out the interest form.
