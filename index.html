Bezier - A Montreal Grouphouse

We—a novice bit-bashing telescope engineer and an apprentice evoker of dreams—are starting a group house in Montreal! This will be a space for nerds to exchange ideas—or rather, for ideas to take the leap across the chasm between two minds. To this end, we call for futurists of all sorts (artists, engineers, scientists, etc.) to gather at our house once per week for dinner and demo nights.

When? Sundays, starting in September.
Where? Montreal, address not yet extant.
Who? You!
